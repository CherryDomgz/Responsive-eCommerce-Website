SHIPPING

At Peaceful Life Shoppe, we are committed to ship out your orders as fast as we can.

All orders placed before 2:30 PM EST will be shipped out the same day (Except Weekends & Holidays). Orders placed during the weekends or holidays will likely be processed by Canada Post on the following Monday or next business day. Please note tracking numbers may take up to 48 hours to be live within Canada Post.

  • All shipping rates are in Canadian Dollars (CAD).
  • Free Expedited shipping on Canadian orders over $60.00 CAD.
  • Free Xpresspost shipping on Canadian orders over $120.00 CAD.
  • Free Tracked Packet shipping on U.S. orders over $75.00 CAD.
  • All orders will be shipped via Canada Post.
  • All orders will be tracked via Canada Post.
  • P.O Box address must be able to accept parcels.

Shipping Policy

Please ensure the following prior to your order:

  • Correct Shipping and Billing Address.
  • Correct phone number.
  • Aware of your local customs regulations (Outside Canada).

We are not responsible if:

  • Packages are stolen and damaged during transportation by the carrier.
  • Packages are refused by the local custom.
  • Packages are delayed due to further custom examination.
  • Packages are delayed due to the holiday season.
